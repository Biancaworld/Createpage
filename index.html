How to Create Your Own Web Page In GitHub

Step 1

Register an account on Github.com

Step 2

Log into your Github account. Look at your repositories. Click on "New" and give repository name as Repositoryname.github.io

Step 3

Create your html file on your editor.

Step 4

Upload your existing file to your repositories and click Commit changes button.

Step 5

Type your repository name on a browser and go, you will see the page that you just loaded.
